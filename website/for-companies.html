Mentors

Deep expertise. Time to listen. Impartial advice.

Deep expertise

We work with retired senior leaders who built your industry. Former partners, directors, and executives. The people who actually made the decisions, not generalist coaches who learned it from a podcast.

Time to listen

Your senior team is operating, not mentoring. A retired veteran has the time to read your junior's draft carefully, follow up between calls, and give the kind of attention mentoring actually needs.

Impartial advice

No consulting pitch at the end. No quiet recruiting from your team. Retired veterans show up because they want to, not because the relationship leads somewhere transactional.

Mission

Every seat funds a free seat.

For every junior in your programme, Mantle funds the same mentorship for one young person currently out of work. No application from you. No box to tick. It is built into how we price.
