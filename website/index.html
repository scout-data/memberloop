How it works

Three steps. No CV.
No cover letter. No introductions.

01

Pick a challenge

Real briefs set by Mantle or sponsored by companies hiring. Pick one you can do. Use any tools you want, including AI. Submit your work before the deadline.

02

Make the leaderboard

Submissions are rubric-scored. The leaderboard is public. The top 20 are invited to defend their work live to a panel of retired industry veterans.

03

Get the interview

Top performers from the live defence go straight to employer interviews. The leaderboard is your portfolio. The judges' verdict is your reference.
